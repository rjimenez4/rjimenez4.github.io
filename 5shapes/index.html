Coldplay Artistic Research Project

An interdisciplinary exploration of music, technology, and creativity

Project Overview

This multidisciplinary research project involves students from different age groups and specialties exploring various aspects of Coldplay's music, career, and impact through creative and technological projects.

Project Highlights

Students have proposed innovative ideas to make concerts more sustainable, including smart clothing, augmented reality, and energy-generating devices. They've also developed audio visualizers for Coldplay songs featuring interactive elements.
